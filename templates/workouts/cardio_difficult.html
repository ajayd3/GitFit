Difficult Cardio Workouts

This will be a high intensity interval training cardio workout. The total time required to complete this workout is 25 minutes

1. Warm Up: Brisk walk or jog at a comfortable pace for 5 minutes. 2. Set your speed and incline to a very challenging pace. 3. We recommend between 9 and 11 mph and incline from 1.0 to 4.0. Go all out sprinting for 30 seconds. 4. Lower the speed to 3.5 to 4 mph and walk for one minute. This is your recovery period. 5. Repeat the cycle 9 more times for a total of 10 cycles. 6. Cool down: Walk at your recovery speed for five minutes to cool down.
